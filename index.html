About McAvoy Consulting

Three Decades of
Engineering Expertise

A specialist technical recruiting firm with a proven record of connecting exceptional engineers with industry-leading companies across North America.

Focused Entirely on the Engineering Environment

Since 1990, McAvoy Consulting has operated exclusively within the engineering space. That singular focus is our competitive advantage — it means we speak your technical language and understand what "right fit" actually looks like for a hardware or firmware team.

We serve the hiring needs of tech startups to Fortune 100 companies, from a single critical hire to the full staffing of engineering teams. Our direct-vetting approach means we don't send you a pile of resumes — we send you the right people.

Over three decades, we've built trusted relationships with leaders across Electrical, Mechanical, Software/Firmware, Industrial, and RF engineering disciplines, among others.
